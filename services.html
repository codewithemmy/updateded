Our SERVICES

By available records both nationally and internationally, 90percent of Nigeria's gross earnings come from oil and gas activities. this includes explorations, exploitations and marketings. Not unxpectedly, environmental challenges also come with it. Our law firm cannot rightly ignore these, moreso with our background and training in the oil rich city of Port Harcourt, from where our skills began. Over the years, we have represented organisations in preparing contracts, negotiating appropriate compensations and remedial measures in cases of oil spillages amongst many other professional services.

Oil, Gas and environment is a specie of property. The other specie is real property. Many times, activities of the former impacts on, sprouts from and into the latter. Often, such inquiries as the applicable laws, appropriate tittles are common place. Additionally, sometimes workers and their skills, including their proper deployment occupy a prime stage in organisations. Such specific issues as industrial accidents, employment and wages, lay offs and redundancies could often be on the spot where our services are often called upon for use.

Among members of the business community, it is common knowledge that Nigeria's economy is fragile. The fragility here is a consequence of both weak macro and micro factors. The inevitable sequence becomes the need to factor in possible business failures and possible exit routes that guarantee minimum scars in each possible business scenario. This is our meaning of Insolvencies. Our knowledge, skills, network of relationships and experience garnared over a period three decades make our firm outstanding in this area of the law. Some of our clients include Assets Management Corporation of Nigeria1 (AMCON) which is the biggest wharehouse in Nigeria.

Since the celebrated English case of Salomon vs Salomon, a body of laws, rules and regulations have emerged in the bid to keep the ever expanding world of business within legal limits. Some are national, while others are international. Alongside this is an equally expanding tax rule designed to guarantee revenues for sovereign governments. Our services include guiding organisations to keep faith with these ever expanding laws while guaranteeing returns to thier shareholders.

Organisations operate and make profits by what they stand for, invent and propagate. Regularly, we are called upon to protect them from unauthorized parties. This is what we have done creditably well for our clients over the years. Would you not rather benefit from this pool of experience?

In recent years, court room litigations, with all their drawbacks are losing attraction for a growing number of business organizations. This is worse in developing economies where they are becoming increasingly less attractive on account of it's many limitations. Our law firm recognises this and thus encourages our clients to explore the many advantages of Arbitration. In this direction, we are equipped for this, being a very important tool in the 21st century business world.
